Welcome to Lonely-Lobster

Simulating the effects of sharing critical resources over multiple value chains





Have you ever wondered why work flows so well organized in industrial production lines, however pretty rarely in the office where specialists need to cooperate?
Then, Lonely-Lobster might be for you!
Lonely-Lobster allows you to model multiple value chains, control influx of new work, assign workers to the process steps and even share them between various process steps. Then run a simulation and observe how the workers learn to select the best strategies which work item to pick next. You want to apply work-in-progress limits to steamline the flow through the value chains? Do it yourself or let Lonely-Lobster help you to find good settings. Play around with the parameters and see how they influence statistical data such as the overall performance.
